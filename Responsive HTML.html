Tom and Jerry

A T.V. show about a cat who's unsuccessful at hunting down a mouse

The names of the two Main Characters in the show:

  • Tom the blue cat
  • Jerry the brown mouse

Some names of the Side Characters in the show:

  • Mammy Two Shoes the house maid
  • Spike the gray dog
  • Tyke the gray puppy
  • Nibbles the gray mouse
  • Quacker the yellow duckling
  • Butch the black cat
  • Toodles Galore the white cat
